# Learn Without Limits CIC – Blog & Knowledge Base

This repository contains the Learn Without Limits CIC blog and knowledge base.

It captures lived experience, real-world scenarios, and practical guidance to support families navigating the Additional Learning Needs (ALN) system in Wales.

## Ownership

The platform, structure, and knowledge system are developed and owned by Golau Insight Ltd.

Learn Without Limits CIC operates the platform under licence for public benefit delivery.

## Contributions

Articles may be written by a range of contributors, including parents, professionals, and community members.

Contributors retain authorship of their work while granting Learn Without Limits CIC permission to publish and use their content as part of the programme.

## Purpose

The blog forms part of a wider ecosystem designed to:

- Provide accessible, practical guidance
- Translate lived experience into usable knowledge
- Build a shared understanding of real-world challenges
- Support earlier intervention and better outcomes

## Status

This is a live and evolving knowledge base, continuously expanding through community input and programme development.
