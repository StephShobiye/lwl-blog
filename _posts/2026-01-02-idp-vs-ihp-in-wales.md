<h3 data-end="376" data-start="286"><em data-end="376" data-start="290">A Clear Guide for Parents on the Difference Between Education Plans and Health Plans</em></h3>
<p data-end="597" data-start="378">Many parents in <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">Wales</a> read advice online that is written for families in <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=England&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">England</a>. This causes understandable confusion because the <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=English+EHCP+system&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">English EHCP system</a> is very different from the <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Welsh+Additional+Learning+Needs+system&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">Welsh Additional Learning Needs system</a>.</p>
<p data-end="751" data-start="599">One of the most common misunderstandings is the difference between an <strong data-end="706" data-start="669">Individual Development Plan (IDP)</strong> and an <strong data-end="750" data-start="714">Individual Healthcare Plan (IHP)</strong>.</p>
<p data-end="961" data-start="753">These two documents serve very different purposes, follow different laws and offer different protections. Here is a simple clear guide explaining what each one does and when your child might need one or both.</p>
<hr data-end="966" data-start="963" />
<h1 data-end="1000" data-start="968"><strong data-end="1000" data-start="970">1. What is an IDP in Wales</strong></h1>
<p data-end="1209" data-start="1002">The <strong data-end="1043" data-start="1006">Individual Development Plan (IDP)</strong> is the legal education plan for children and young people with Additional Learning Needs under the <em data-end="1208" data-start="1143"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Additional+Learning+Needs+and+Education+Tribunal+Wales+Act+2018&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">Additional Learning Needs and Education Tribunal Wales Act 2018</a></em>.</p>
<h3 data-end="1251" data-start="1211">✔ It is a legally binding document</h3>
<h3 data-end="1304" data-start="1252">✔ It can be enforced at the Education Tribunal</h3>
<h3 data-end="1360" data-start="1305">✔ It replaces Statements of SEN and IEPs in Wales</h3>
<p data-end="1374" data-start="1362">An IDP must:</p>
<ul data-end="1760" data-start="1376">
<li data-end="1424" data-start="1376">
<p data-end="1424" data-start="1378">Identify a child’s additional learning needs</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1460" data-start="1425">
<p data-end="1460" data-start="1427">Describe the barriers they face</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1519" data-start="1461">
<p data-end="1519" data-start="1463">Detail the support required to overcome those barriers</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1571" data-start="1520">
<p data-end="1571" data-start="1522">Specify who delivers each part of the provision</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1639" data-start="1572">
<p data-end="1639" data-start="1574">Explain how often and in what way the support must be delivered</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1671" data-start="1640">
<p data-end="1671" data-start="1642">Include measurable outcomes</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1705" data-start="1672">
<p data-end="1705" data-start="1674">Be reviewed at least annually</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1760" data-start="1706">
<p data-end="1760" data-start="1708">Be maintained by the school or the local authority</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="1825" data-start="1762">The IDP is about <strong data-end="1824" data-start="1779">learning, access and educational progress</strong>.</p>
<p data-end="1966" data-start="1827">It is similar to an <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=EHCP+in+England&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">EHCP in England</a>, but it is not the same and parents should NEVER rely on English EHCP guidance when understanding IDPs.<br /><br />For more information about what should be included in an IDP, please see our previous article here&nbsp;<a href="https://learnwithoutlimitscic.blogspot.com/2025/11/idp-series-what-must-be-included-in-idp.html">https://learnwithoutlimitscic.blogspot.com/2025/11/idp-series-what-must-be-included-in-idp.html</a></p>
<hr data-end="1971" data-start="1968" />
<h1 data-end="2005" data-start="1973"><strong data-end="2005" data-start="1975">2. What is an IHP in Wales</strong></h1>
<p data-end="2202" data-start="2007">The <strong data-end="2047" data-start="2011">Individual Healthcare Plan (IHP)</strong> is the document used to support a child’s medical needs in school.<br data-end="2117" data-start="2114" />
It follows the Welsh Government guidance <em data-end="2201" data-start="2158"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Supporting+Learners+with+Healthcare+Needs+Welsh+Government+guidance&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">Supporting Learners with Healthcare Needs</a></em>.</p>
<h3 data-end="2246" data-start="2204">✔ It is a health and safety document</h3>
<h3 data-end="2296" data-start="2247">✔ It is not legally enforceable at tribunal</h3>
<h3 data-end="2355" data-start="2297">✔ It is created with input from health professionals</h3>
<p data-end="2383" data-start="2357">A good IHP should include:</p>
<ul data-end="2804" data-start="2385">
<li data-end="2418" data-start="2385">
<p data-end="2418" data-start="2387">The child’s medical condition</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2458" data-start="2419">
<p data-end="2458" data-start="2421">Symptoms warning signs and triggers</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2501" data-start="2459">
<p data-end="2501" data-start="2461">What to do if the child becomes unwell</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2548" data-start="2502">
<p data-end="2548" data-start="2504">Medication names doses timings and storage</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2592" data-start="2549">
<p data-end="2592" data-start="2551">Who is trained to administer medication</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2617" data-start="2593">
<p data-end="2617" data-start="2595">Emergency procedures</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2651" data-start="2618">
<p data-end="2651" data-start="2620">Hydration and nutrition needs</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2694" data-start="2652">
<p data-end="2694" data-start="2654">Rest break or sensory regulation needs</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2754" data-start="2695">
<p data-end="2754" data-start="2697">When the child is considered too unwell to be in school</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2804" data-start="2755">
<p data-end="2804" data-start="2757">Communication methods between school and home</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="2865" data-start="2806">The IHP is about <strong data-end="2849" data-start="2823">health care and safety</strong>, not education.</p>

<h1 data-end="3478" data-start="3444"><strong data-end="3478" data-start="3446">4. When a Child Needs an IDP</strong></h1>
<p data-end="3557" data-start="3480">A child needs an IDP when they have an <strong data-end="3547" data-start="3519">additional learning need</strong>, meaning:</p>
<ul data-end="3770" data-start="3559">
<li data-end="3608" data-start="3559">
<p data-end="3608" data-start="3561">they have a learning difficulty or disability</p>
</li>
<li data-end="3658" data-start="3609">
<p data-end="3658" data-start="3611">the difficulty impacts their ability to learn</p>
</li>
<li data-end="3722" data-start="3659">
<p data-end="3722" data-start="3661">they need special educational provision to access education</p>
</li>
<li data-end="3770" data-start="3723">
<p data-end="3770" data-start="3725">reasonable adjustments alone are not enough</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="3789" data-start="3772">This can include:</p>
<ul data-end="4049" data-start="3791">
<li data-end="3801" data-start="3791">
<p data-end="3801" data-start="3793"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=autism&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">autism</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="3810" data-start="3802">
<p data-end="3810" data-start="3804"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=ADHD&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">ADHD</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="3823" data-start="3811">
<p data-end="3823" data-start="3813"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=dyslexia&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">dyslexia</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="3853" data-start="3824">
<p data-end="3853" data-start="3826"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=speech+and+language+needs&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">speech and language needs</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="3887" data-start="3854">
<p data-end="3887" data-start="3856"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=anxiety+and+learning&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">anxiety that affects learning</a></p>

<p data-end="3925" data-start="3890"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=emotional+regulation+difficulties&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">emotional regulation difficulties</a></p>
</li>

</li>

<p data-end="3981" data-start="3952"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=sensory+processing+disorder&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=286811146964177772" target="_blank">sensory processing disorder</a></p>

<h1 data-end="4171" data-start="4137"><strong data-end="4171" data-start="4139">5. When a Child Needs an IHP</strong></h1>
<p data-end="4199" data-start="4173">A child needs an IHP when:</p>
<ul data-end="4537" data-start="4201">
<li data-end="4241" data-start="4201">
<p data-end="4241" data-start="4203">they have a chronic health condition</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4276" data-start="4242">
<p data-end="4276" data-start="4244">they need medication in school</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4314" data-start="4277">
<p data-end="4314" data-start="4279">they require emergency procedures</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4369" data-start="4315">
<p data-end="4369" data-start="4317">they need staff to be trained in health care tasks</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4423" data-start="4370">
<p data-end="4423" data-start="4372">they need hydration rest or nutrition adjustments</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4475" data-start="4424">
<p data-end="4475" data-start="4426">their condition fluctuates and needs monitoring</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4537" data-start="4476">
<p data-end="4537" data-start="4478">their wellbeing or safety would be at risk without a plan</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="4556" data-start="4539">Examples include:</p>

<li data-end="4568" data-start="4558">

<h1 data-end="4908" data-start="4858"><strong data-end="4908" data-start="4860">6. The Most Common Misunderstanding in Wales</strong></h1>
<p data-end="4933" data-start="4910">Parents are often told:</p>
<blockquote data-end="5004" data-start="4935">
<p data-end="5004" data-start="4937">“Your child does not need an IDP because they have a medical plan.”</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-end="5030" data-start="5006">This is <strong data-end="5029" data-start="5014">not correct</strong>.</p>
<p data-end="5070" data-start="5032">If a child’s medical condition causes:</p>
<ul data-end="5281" data-start="5072">
<li data-end="5083" data-start="5072">

<h1 data-end="5430" data-start="5390"><strong data-end="5430" data-start="5392">7. How the Two Plans Work Together</strong></h1>
<p data-end="5454" data-start="5432">Think of it like this:</p>
<h3 data-end="5504" data-start="5456"><strong data-end="5502" data-start="5460">The IDP supports the child’s learning.</strong></h3>
<h3 data-end="5549" data-start="5505"><strong data-end="5549" data-start="5509">The IHP supports the child’s health.</strong></h3>
<p data-end="5597" data-start="5551">They are separate but must inform one another.</p>
<p data-end="5808" data-start="5599">If a child has a medical condition, the IHP explains what the condition is and what support is needed.<br data-end="5704" data-start="5701" />
If that condition impacts learning or attendance, the IDP explains what educational support is required.</p>
<p data-end="5888" data-start="5810">Most children with both ALN and medical needs require <strong data-end="5887" data-start="5864">both plans together</strong>.<br /><br />For more information about how exactly health information feeds into the IDP please read our previous article here&nbsp;<a href="https://learnwithoutlimitscic.blogspot.com/2025/11/idp-series-health-social-care.html">https://learnwithoutlimitscic.blogspot.com/2025/11/idp-series-health-social-care.html</a><br /><br />If your child has a private diagnosis, we've explained how that feeds into the IDP process,&nbsp;<a href="https://learnwithoutlimitscic.blogspot.com/2025/11/idp-series-private-diagnoses-and-idp.html" target="_blank">Private Diagnoses and IDP</a></p>

<h1 data-end="5950" data-start="5895"><strong data-end="5950" data-start="5897">8. For Families Who Have Read English EHCP Advice</strong></h1>
<p data-end="5974" data-start="5952">English EHCPs combine:</p>
<ul data-end="6016" data-start="5976">
<li data-end="5989" data-start="5976">
<p data-end="5989" data-start="5978">education</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6000" data-start="5990">
<p data-end="6000" data-start="5992">health</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6016" data-start="6001">
<p data-end="6016" data-start="6003">social care</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="6047" data-start="6018">into a single legal document.</p>
<p data-end="6076" data-start="6049">Wales does not.<br data-end="6067" data-start="6064" />
In Wales:</p>
<ul data-end="6248" data-start="6078">
<li data-end="6115" data-start="6078">
<p data-end="6115" data-start="6080">The <strong data-end="6091" data-start="6084">IDP</strong> is the legal document</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6166" data-start="6116">
<p data-end="6166" data-start="6118">The <strong data-end="6129" data-start="6122">IHP</strong> is the supporting medical guidance</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6248" data-start="6167">
<p data-end="6248" data-start="6169">Health support must be coordinated but is not legally binding in the same way</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="6316" data-start="6250">This is why England based advice does not map to the Welsh system.</p>

<h1 data-end="6374" data-start="6323"><strong data-end="6374" data-start="6325">9. When to Request an IDP Review or a New IDP</strong></h1>
<p data-end="6413" data-start="6376">Consider requesting an IDP review if:</p>
<ul data-end="6784" data-start="6415">
<li data-end="6458" data-start="6415">
<p data-end="6458" data-start="6417">health needs begin to affect attendance</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6502" data-start="6459">
<p data-end="6502" data-start="6461">your child needs adjustments in lessons</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6554" data-start="6503">
<p data-end="6554" data-start="6505">your child is too fatigued to manage a full day</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6590" data-start="6555">
<p data-end="6590" data-start="6557">behaviour worsens after illness</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6646" data-start="6591">
<p data-end="6646" data-start="6593">anxiety around health becomes a barrier to learning</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6713" data-start="6647">
<p data-end="6713" data-start="6649">the school is relying only on the IHP and not updating the IDP</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6784" data-start="6714">
<p data-end="6784" data-start="6716">a slow recovery after flu or a viral illness is affecting learning</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="6838" data-start="6786">Requesting a review is your right under the ALN Act.</p>
