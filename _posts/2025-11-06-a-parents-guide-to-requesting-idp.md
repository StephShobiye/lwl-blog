<h3 data-end="1079" data-start="1037">💬 <strong data-end="1079" data-start="1044">Why Requesting a Review Matters</strong></h3>
<p data-end="1319" data-start="1081">An <strong data-end="1121" data-start="1084">Individual Development Plan (IDP)</strong> should evolve with your child.<br />
If their needs change, progress stalls, or current support isn’t working, you have the <em>right</em> to ask for a review.<br />
It’s not confrontation — it’s collaboration.</p>
