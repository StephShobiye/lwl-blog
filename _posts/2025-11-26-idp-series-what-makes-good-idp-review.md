<h1 data-end="1189" data-start="1141"><strong data-end="1189" data-start="1143">1. Legal Duties Under the ALN Act and Code</strong></h1>
<h2 data-end="1243" data-start="1191"><strong data-end="1241" data-start="1194">A) Parents can request a review at ANY time</strong></h2>
<p data-end="1401" data-start="1244"><strong data-end="1262" data-start="1244">ALN Code 25.21</strong> gives parents an unrestricted right to request an IDP review.<br data-end="1327" data-start="1324" />
The school or LA <strong data-end="1352" data-start="1344">must</strong> carry out the review unless it is â€œunnecessaryâ€.</p>
<hr data-end="1406" data-start="1403" />
<h2 data-end="1450" data-start="1408"><strong data-end="1448" data-start="1411">B) Reviews must be person-centred</strong></h2>
<p data-end="1514" data-start="1451">The process must adapt to the child, &amp; not the other way around.</p>
<hr data-end="1519" data-start="1516" />
<h2 data-end="1580" data-start="1521"><strong data-end="1580" data-start="1524">C) Reviews must check whether ALP is being delivered</strong></h2>
<p data-end="1628" data-start="1582"><strong data-end="1621" data-start="1582">Additional Learning Provision (ALP)</strong> means:</p>
<blockquote data-end="1756" data-start="1630">
<p data-end="1756" data-start="1632">The extra support a child needs to learn, which is additional to or different from what is normally available to all pupils.</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-end="1772" data-start="1758">This includes:</p>
<ul data-end="1884" data-start="1774">
<li data-end="1799" data-start="1774">
<p data-end="1799" data-start="1776">educational provision</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1828" data-start="1800">
<p data-end="1828" data-start="1802">health-related provision</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1884" data-start="1829">
<p data-end="1884" data-start="1831">social-care-related provision that affects learning</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="1925" data-start="1886">The review must examine whether ALP is:</p>
<ul data-end="2092" data-start="1927">
<li data-end="1946" data-start="1927">
<p data-end="1946" data-start="1929">being delivered</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1960" data-start="1947">
<p data-end="1960" data-start="1949">effective</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1977" data-start="1961">
<p data-end="1977" data-start="1963">still needed</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2016" data-start="1978">
<p data-end="2016" data-start="1980">needing to be changed or increased</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2092" data-start="2017">
<p data-end="2092" data-start="2019">beyond the schoolâ€™s capacity (which triggers LA takeover: ALN Code 23.67)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr data-end="2097" data-start="2094" />
<h2 data-end="2151" data-start="2099"><strong data-end="2149" data-start="2102">D) Reviews must be multi-agency when needed</strong></h2>
<p data-end="2225" data-start="2152">Health and social care must contribute when their input affects learning.</p>

<hr data-end="2763" data-start="2760" />

<p data-end="2834" data-start="2824">The ALNCo:</p>

<h2 data-end="4571" data-start="4526">â­ <strong data-end="4571" data-start="4531">A) When the IDP is school-maintained</strong></h2>
