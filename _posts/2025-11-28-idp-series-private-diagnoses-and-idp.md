<p data-end="583" data-start="435">Families across Wales often turn to private assessments because <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=NHS+waiting+lists&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">NHS waiting lists</a> are long, inconsistent or inaccessible. <p data-end="942" data-start="833">This article explains — calmly, clearly, and legally — how private reports should be used in the <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=IDP+process+Wales+education&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">IDP process</a>.</p>

<h1 data-end="997" data-start="949"><strong data-end="995" data-start="951">1. Are Private Diagnoses Valid for IDPs?</strong></h1>
<h3 data-end="1067" data-start="998"><strong data-end="1067" data-start="1002">Yes. 100% yes. They are legally valid and must be considered.</strong></h3>
<p data-end="1089" data-start="1069">The ALN Code states:</p>
<blockquote data-end="1386" data-start="1091">
<p data-end="1386" data-start="1093"><strong data-end="1290" data-start="1093">“Identifying whether a child… has ALN requires evidence…<br data-end="1154" data-start="1151" />
This evidence might come from… other services…<br data-end="1205" data-start="1202" />
It might also come from the child, their parents or the young person themselves.”</strong><br data-end="1293" data-start="1290" />
(Cod ADY 20.2)<br data-end="1310" data-start="1307" />
<strong data-end="1369" data-start="1312">“This evidence may also include specialist services.”</strong><br data-end="1372" data-start="1369" />
(Cod ADY 20.3)</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-end="1414" data-start="1388">This includes <strong data-end="1413" data-start="1402">private</strong>:</p>
<ul data-end="1598" data-start="1416">
<li data-end="1435" data-start="1416">
<p data-end="1435" data-start="1418"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=ASD+assessments&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">ASD assessments</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="1456" data-start="1436">
<p data-end="1456" data-start="1438"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=ADHD+assessments&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">ADHD assessments</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="1489" data-start="1457">
<p data-end="1489" data-start="1459"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Dyslexia+dyscalculia+reports+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">Dyslexia/dyscalculia reports</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="1508" data-start="1490">

<h3 data-end="1651" data-start="1600">✔ Schools/LAs must consider private evidence.</h3>
<h3 data-end="1721" data-start="1652">✔ There is no legal distinction between private and NHS evidence.</h3>
<hr data-end="1726" data-start="1723" />
<h1 data-end="1776" data-start="1728"><strong data-end="1774" data-start="1730">2. Schools Cannot Reject Private Reports</strong></h1>
<p data-end="1833" data-start="1777">Schools must base decisions on <em data-end="1813" data-start="1808">all</em> evidence presented.</p>
<p data-end="1857" data-start="1835">The ALN Code requires:</p>
<blockquote data-end="1916" data-start="1859">
<p data-end="1916" data-start="1861"><strong data-end="1897" data-start="1861">“…using the evidence available…”</strong><br data-end="1900" data-start="1897" />
(ALN Code 20.19)</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-end="1921" data-start="1918">and</p>
<blockquote data-end="2015" data-start="1923">
<p data-end="2015" data-start="1925"><strong data-end="1996" data-start="1925">“…seeking advice from appropriate specialist services when needed.”</strong><br data-end="1999" data-start="1996" />
(ALN Code 20.20)</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-end="2052" data-start="2017">This creates a legal obligation to:</p>
<p data-end="2214" data-start="2054">✔ Consider private EP assessments<br data-end="2090" data-start="2087" />
✔ Consider private ASD/ADHD reports<br data-end="2128" data-start="2125" />
✔ Consider private sensory/OT/SALT reports<br data-end="2173" data-start="2170" />
✔ Consider independent medical evidence</p>
<p data-end="2249" data-start="2216">A school <strong data-end="2235" data-start="2225">cannot</strong> lawfully say:</p>
<p data-end="2373" data-start="2251">❌ “We don’t accept private reports.”<br data-end="2290" data-start="2287" />
❌ “Only CAMHS can diagnose ASD.”<br data-end="2325" data-start="2322" />
❌ “This doesn’t count unless NHS confirms it.”</p>
<p data-end="2432" data-start="2375">They may disagree with parts of a report — but they must:</p>
<ul data-end="2517" data-start="2434">
<li data-end="2462" data-start="2434">
<p data-end="2462" data-start="2436">record the evidence, and</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2517" data-start="2463">
<p data-end="2517" data-start="2465">provide justification if they take a different view.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="2541" data-start="2519">They cannot ignore it.</p>
<hr data-end="2546" data-start="2543" />
<h1 data-end="2611" data-start="2548"><strong data-end="2611" data-start="2550">3. Local Authorities Cannot Ignore Private Reports Either</strong></h1>
<p data-end="2645" data-start="2613"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Local+Authorities+ALN+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">Local Authorities</a> must consider:</p>
<ul data-end="2760" data-start="2647">
<li data-end="2685" data-start="2647">
<p data-end="2685" data-start="2649">all relevant evidence (20.2, 20.3)</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2718" data-start="2686">
<p data-end="2718" data-start="2688">all parental evidence (20.2)</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2760" data-start="2719">
<p data-end="2760" data-start="2721">all specialist evidence (20.3, 20.20)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="2854" data-start="2762">If the LA feels more assessment is required, they must <strong data-end="2828" data-start="2817">seek it</strong>, not dismiss what exists.</p>
<p data-end="2873" data-start="2856">This may include:</p>
<ul data-end="2953" data-start="2875">
<li data-end="2894" data-start="2875">
<p data-end="2894" data-start="2877"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=NHS+paediatrics+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">NHS paediatrics</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="2910" data-start="2895">
<p data-end="2910" data-start="2897"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=NHS+SALT+OT+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">NHS SALT/OT</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="2920" data-start="2911">
<p data-end="2920" data-start="2913"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=LA+educational+psychologist+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">LA EP</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="2953" data-start="2921">
<p data-end="2953" data-start="2923"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=specialist+advisory+teachers+role&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">specialist advisory teachers</a></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="3024" data-start="2955">But private evidence still <strong data-end="3023" data-start="2982">forms part of the legal evidence base</strong>.</p>
<hr data-end="3029" data-start="3026" />
<h1 data-end="3095" data-start="3031"><strong data-end="3095" data-start="3033">4. What If a Private Diagnosis Conflicts with NHS Opinion?</strong></h1>
<p data-end="3281" data-start="3097">Contradictions happen.<br data-end="3122" data-start="3119" />
Private ASD: NHS says “not ASD”.<br data-end="3157" data-start="3154" />
NHS says “anxiety”: private EP identifies ASD/ADHD traits.<br data-end="3218" data-start="3215" />
One SALT says “no need”: private SALT says “significant delay”.</p>
<p data-end="3304" data-start="3283">The ALN Code expects:</p>
<ul data-end="3469" data-start="3306">
<li data-end="3351" data-start="3306">
<p data-end="3351" data-start="3308">consideration of <strong data-end="3332" data-start="3325">all</strong> evidence (20.19)</p>
</li>
<li data-end="3414" data-start="3352">
<p data-end="3414" data-start="3354">seeking clarification from appropriate specialists (20.20)</p>
</li>
<li data-end="3469" data-start="3415">
<p data-end="3469" data-start="3417">decisions based on <strong data-end="3455" data-start="3436"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=define+functional+need+education&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">functional need</a></strong>, not labels</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 data-end="3519" data-start="3471">✔ The IDP is based on <em data-end="3503" data-start="3497">need</em>, not diagnosis.</h3>
<p data-end="3577" data-start="3521">Even if professionals disagree, functional needs remain:</p>
<ul data-end="3707" data-start="3579">
<li data-end="3596" data-start="3579">
<p data-end="3596" data-start="3581">communication</p>
</li>
<li data-end="3614" data-start="3597">
<p data-end="3614" data-start="3599"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=dysregulation+child+development&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">dysregulation</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="3634" data-start="3615">
<p data-end="3634" data-start="3617"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=sensory+profile+child+development&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">sensory profile</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="3660" data-start="3635">
<p data-end="3660" data-start="3637"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=executive+functioning+skills&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">executive functioning</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="3685" data-start="3661">
<p data-end="3685" data-start="3663">social communication</p>
</li>
<li data-end="3707" data-start="3686">
<p data-end="3707" data-start="3688">learning barriers</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="3788" data-start="3709">The IDP must reflect what the child <strong data-end="3754" data-start="3745">needs</strong>, regardless of diagnostic debate.</p>

<li data-end="4654" data-start="4634">

<p data-end="4851" data-start="4829">specialist placement</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4861" data-start="4852">
<p data-end="4861" data-start="4854"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=EOTAS+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">EOTAS</a></p>

<li data-end="4887" data-start="4862">
<p data-end="4887" data-start="4864">adjustments in school</p>

<h1 data-end="4949" data-start="4894"><strong data-end="4949" data-start="4896">6. How to Submit a Private Report (Parent Script)</strong></h1>
<p data-end="5135" data-start="4951"><strong data-end="4963" data-start="4951">ENGLISH:</strong><br data-end="4966" data-start="4963" />
“I would like this private assessment to be recorded as evidence under ALN Code 20.2 and 20.3.<br data-end="5063" data-start="5060" />
Please confirm in writing that it will be considered in the IDP review.”</p>
<p data-end="5328" data-start="5137"><strong data-end="5149" data-start="5137">CYMRAEG:</strong><br data-end="5152" data-start="5149" />
“Hoffwn i’r asesiad preifat hwn gael ei gofnodi fel tystiolaeth o dan God ADY 20.2 a 20.3.<br data-end="5245" data-start="5242" />
A allech gadarnhau yn ysgrifenedig y bydd yn cael ei ystyried yn yr adolygiad CTP?”</p>
<p data-end="5345" data-start="5330">If they resist:</p>
<p data-end="5467" data-start="5347"><strong data-end="5467" data-start="5347">“The ALN Code requires decisions to consider all relevant evidence (20.19).<br data-end="5427" data-start="5424" />
Can you put your position in writing?”</strong></p>
<p data-end="5538" data-start="5469">Very few will do so — because they know they can’t legally refuse it.</p>
<hr data-end="5543" data-start="5540" />
<h1 data-end="5569" data-start="5545"><strong data-end="5569" data-start="5547">7. Myths vs Truths</strong></h1>
<h3 data-end="5610" data-start="5571">❌ “We only accept NHS diagnoses.”</h3>
<p data-end="5675" data-start="5611">✔ The Code requires <em data-end="5636" data-start="5631">all</em> relevant evidence (20.2, 20.3, 20.19).</p>
<h3 data-end="5719" data-start="5677">❌ “Private EP reports aren’t valid.”</h3>
<p data-end="5782" data-start="5720">✔ EP advice is specialist advice (20.20) → must be considered.</p>
<h3 data-end="5823" data-start="5784">❌ “We need CAMHS to agree first.”</h3>
<p data-end="5887" data-start="5824">✔ CAMHS is <em data-end="5840" data-start="5835">not</em> the decision-maker for IDPs (schools/LAs are).</p>
<h3 data-end="5935" data-start="5889">❌ “We’ll wait for NHS assessment first.”</h3>
<p data-end="6009" data-start="5936">✔ The IDP must reflect <strong data-end="5976" data-start="5959">current needs</strong>, not planned future assessments.</p>
<hr data-end="6014" data-start="6011" />
<h1 data-end="6074" data-start="6016"><strong data-end="6074" data-start="6018">8. When Private Reports Trigger an LA-Maintained IDP</strong></h1>
<p data-end="6159" data-start="6076">Sometimes private assessments reveal needs that <strong data-end="6158" data-start="6124">schools cannot reasonably meet</strong>.</p>
<p data-end="6170" data-start="6161">Examples:</p>
<ul data-end="6440" data-start="6172">
<li data-end="6197" data-start="6172">
<p data-end="6197" data-start="6174">complex sensory needs</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6232" data-start="6198">
<p data-end="6232" data-start="6200"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=EBSA+linked+to+severe+distress+in+schools&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=805982029813660010" target="_blank">EBSA linked to severe distress</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="6243" data-start="6233">
<p data-end="6243" data-start="6235">trauma</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6287" data-start="6244">
<p data-end="6287" data-start="6246">physical disability requiring equipment</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6337" data-start="6288">
<p data-end="6337" data-start="6290">significant dyslexia requiring specialist ALP</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6366" data-start="6338">
<p data-end="6366" data-start="6340">multi-agency involvement</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6404" data-start="6367">
<p data-end="6404" data-start="6369">chronic illness impacting stamina</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6440" data-start="6405">
<p data-end="6440" data-start="6407">substantial mental health needs</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="6471" data-start="6442">The relevant Code section is:</p>
<h3 data-end="6499" data-start="6473">✔ <strong data-end="6497" data-start="6479">ALN Code 23.67</strong></h3>
<p data-end="6615" data-start="6500">If the ALP required <strong data-end="6576" data-start="6520">cannot be delivered in full at any maintained school</strong>, the LA must take over responsibility.</p>
<p data-end="6654" data-start="6617">This is the legally correct citation.</p>
<hr data-end="6659" data-start="6656" />
<h1 data-end="6717" data-start="6661"><strong data-end="6717" data-start="6663">9. When a Private Report Justifies an Early Review</strong></h1>
<p data-end="6760" data-start="6719">Parents may request a review at any time.</p>
<p data-end="6786" data-start="6762">The correct citation is:</p>
<h3 data-end="6814" data-start="6788">✔ <strong data-end="6812" data-start="6794">ALN Code 25.21</strong></h3>
<blockquote data-end="6904" data-start="6815">
<p data-end="6904" data-start="6817">Parents can request a review, and the school/LA <strong data-end="6884" data-start="6865">must conduct it</strong> unless unnecessary.</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-end="7007" data-start="6906"><strong data-end="6924" data-start="6906">Parent script:</strong><br data-end="6927" data-start="6924" />
“I request an early review of the IDP under ALN Code 25.21 due to new evidence.”</p>

<h1 data-end="7070" data-start="7014"><strong data-end="7070" data-start="7016">10. Summary: What Private Reports Mean for the IDP</strong></h1>
<p data-end="7430" data-start="7072">✔ They are legally valid<br data-end="7099" data-start="7096" />
✔ They must be considered<br data-end="7127" data-start="7124" />
✔ Schools cannot reject them<br data-end="7158" data-start="7155" />
✔ LAs cannot ignore them<br data-end="7185" data-start="7182" />
✔ They influence needs, outcomes, ALP and placement<br data-end="7239" data-start="7236" />
✔ They help clarify complex profiles<br data-end="7278" data-start="7275" />
✔ They often support LA-maintained IDPs<br data-end="7320" data-start="7317" />
✔ They strengthen requests for EOTAS when appropriate<br data-end="7376" data-start="7373" />
✔ They help ensure the child’s real needs are captured</p>
