<p>It’s been a while since our last post, but that doesn’t mean we’ve been quiet. Over on our <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Learn+without+Limits+Wales+Facebook+group&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Facebook group</a>, founded in 2013, parents across Wales have been sharing, venting, laughing and problem-solving together. That space, rich with lived experience, has always been the heart of everything we do. This Blog is the official publication arm of Learn without Limits CIC &amp; you can find our main organisational hub, including our Governance and policies here -&nbsp;<a href="https://learnwithoutlimitscic.org/">https://learnwithoutlimitscic.org/</a></p>

<p>Now, we’re taking that spirit forward through <a data-preview="" href="https://learnwithoutlimitscic.org/" target="_blank">Learn without Limits CIC</a> — a parent-led social enterprise built by families, for families. Our mission is simple: to make sure no child with <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=define+Additional+Learning+Needs&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Additional Learning Needs</a> falls through the cracks because a parent couldn’t find the right information or support in time.</p>

<p>We are currently developing an app designed by parents, for parents — a practical companion that answers the questions which so often get lost between services, policies and paperwork. Whether you’re navigating an <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=IDP+education+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">IDP</a>, exploring <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=EOTAS+education&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">EOTAS</a> or balancing learning with life, the app aims to offer clear, compassionate and actionable guidance.</p>

<p>At Learn without Limits CIC, we favour collaboration over competition. We work with a wide range of organisations locally and nationally — including <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Autism%27s+Hidden+Voices&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Autism’s Hidden Voices</a>, <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Autistic+UK+CIC&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Autistic UK CIC</a> and <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Mental+Health+Action+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Mental Health Action Wales</a> — and we engage with whoever we need to in order to get the job done, including government where change is needed.</p>

<p>Our community includes families of children with a wide range of needs: <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=rare+genetic+conditions+affecting+learning&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">rare genetic conditions</a>, visible disabilities such as <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Cerebral+Palsy&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Cerebral Palsy</a> and <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Down+Syndrome&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Down Syndrome</a>, and conditions like <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Dyspraxia&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Dyspraxia</a>, <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Haemophilia&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Haemophilia</a> and <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Autism+Spectrum+Conditions&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">Autism Spectrum Conditions</a>. We also support families whose children are currently out of school or college for reasons such as EBSA (Emotionally Based School Avoidance), mental health challenges or long-term medical treatment, including cancer.</p>

<p>If your child isn’t in school or college right now because their needs aren’t being met, we are your team.</p>

<p>Over the years, we’ve hosted many online peer-support sessions — cups of tea in hand, cameras off or on — and we’ll continue to do so. We also plan to introduce <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=family+support+clinics+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=3398840421349851526" target="_blank">face-to-face drop-in clinics across Wales</a> to make support even more accessible.</p>

<p>This blog will be our space to share reflections, insights and updates — the lived experiences that fuel everything we do. For resources, printables and learning tools, please visit our main website, which acts as the hub connecting our projects and partnerships.</p>
