<li>
<p><strong>Mainstream School</strong>: You can deregister independently with a letter to the headteacher.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Special School</strong>: You must obtain permission from the local authority (LA) before deregistration.</p>
</li>
</ul><p>You can confirm your child‚Äôs school type by contacting the LA or searching on <a href="https://www.estyn.gov.wales/find-a-provider">Estyn‚Äôs provider list</a>.</p><hr /><h2>Step-by-Step: Deregistering from a Mainstream School</h2><ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Write to the Headteacher</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Clearly state your child‚Äôs name, DOB, and your decision to take full responsibility for their education.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Request that they be removed from the admissions register under <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Regulation+8%281%29%28d%29+Education+Pupil+Registration+Wales+Regulations&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=1438653586681525796" target="_blank">Regulation 8(1)(d) of the Education (Pupil Registration) (Wales) Regulations</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ask for confirmation in writing of the removal.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Follow Up</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Schools must remove your child from the register promptly and notify the LA within 10 school days.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If they delay or refuse, politely remind them of their legal obligation and provide a copy of the regulation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Keep a copy of the letter and request written confirmation.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol><hr /><h2>If Your Child Attends a Special School</h2><p>In this case, the process requires <strong>formal consent</strong> from the LA.</p><ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Contact Your LA‚Äôs ALN Department</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Explain your intent to home educate.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Request a meeting or written guidance on next steps.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Secure Consent in Writing</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>The special school cannot remove your child from the roll without this.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Proceed with School Notification</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Once consent is granted, send written notification to the school.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol><hr /><h2>üîÑ Before You Deregister an ALN Child: What to Consider</h2><h3>1. Speak to NHS Therapists Early</h3><p>If your child receives:</p><ul>
