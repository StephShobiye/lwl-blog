<h2 data-end="1081" data-start="983"><strong data-end="1081" data-start="986">How to Request EOTAS, What Happens If the Local Authority Refuses, and What You Can Do Next</strong></h2>
<p data-end="1270" data-start="1083">When a child can no longer access education safely or meaningfully in school, families may reach a point where <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=define+Education+Other+Than+At+School&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">Education Other Than At School</a> (<a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=define+EOTAS&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">EOTAS</a>) becomes the only realistic pathway.</p>
<p data-end="1497" data-start="1272">But knowing <strong data-end="1291" data-start="1284">how</strong> to request EOTAS, <strong data-end="1336" data-start="1310">what evidence you need</strong>, and <strong data-end="1389" data-start="1342">what to do when the <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=local+authority+education+uk&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">Local Authority</a> refuses</strong> is not straightforward. Most parents begin this process exhausted, confused, and with very little guidance.</p>
<p data-end="1674" data-start="1499">This article walks you step by step through the process in <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=education+system+in+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">Wales</a>, using clear language, real experience from families, and the legal duties that Local Authorities must follow.</p>
<hr data-end="1679" data-start="1676" />
<h1 data-end="1720" data-start="1681">‚≠ê When Should a Parent Request EOTAS?</h1>
<p data-end="1828" data-start="1722">EOTAS becomes appropriate when a child <strong data-end="1771" data-start="1761">cannot</strong> receive suitable education in school, even with support.</p>
<p data-end="1844" data-start="1830">This includes:</p>
<ul data-end="2213" data-start="1846">
<li data-end="1903" data-start="1846">
<p data-end="1903" data-start="1848"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=extreme+anxiety&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">extreme anxiety</a> or emotionally based school avoidance</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1924" data-start="1904">
<p data-end="1924" data-start="1906"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=autistic+burnout&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">autistic burnout</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="1958" data-start="1925">
<p data-end="1958" data-start="1927"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Long+Covid&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">Long Covid</a> or <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=chronic+illness+and+school+attendance&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">chronic illness</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="2019" data-start="1959">
<p data-end="2019" data-start="1961">repeated <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=autistic+shutdowns&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">shutdowns</a> or distress linked to the environment</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2085" data-start="2020">
<p data-end="2085" data-start="2022">multiple exclusions or unsafe behaviour driven by unmet needs</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2135" data-start="2086">
<p data-end="2135" data-start="2088"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=sensory+overwhelm&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">sensory overwhelm</a> that cannot be accommodated</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2170" data-start="2136">
<p data-end="2170" data-start="2138">a complete placement breakdown</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2213" data-start="2171">
<p data-end="2213" data-start="2173">no suitable school placement available</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="2280" data-start="2215">A child should not reach a crisis point before EOTAS is considered.</p>

<h1 data-end="2339" data-start="2287">‚≠ê Before Requesting EOTAS: What You Need to Gather</h1>
<p data-end="2450" data-start="2341">Many EOTAS refusals happen because evidence is missing. These are the items that make the biggest difference:</p>

<h1 data-end="3744" data-start="3705">‚≠ê How to Request EOTAS (Step by Step)</h1>
<h2 data-end="3804" data-start="3746">‚≠ê Step 1 ‚Äî Write to the ALNCo and the Local Authority</h2>
<p data-end="3898" data-start="3805">You must make the request <strong data-end="3845" data-start="3831">in writing</strong>.<br data-end="3849" data-start="3846" />
Verbal conversations do not trigger legal duties.</p>
<p data-end="3957" data-start="3900">A template letter is included at the end of this article.</p>
<hr data-end="3962" data-start="3959" />
<h2 data-end="4008" data-start="3964">‚≠ê Step 2 ‚Äî Request an urgent IDP Review</h2>
<p data-end="4112" data-start="4009">EOTAS sits within the IDP process.<br data-end="4046" data-start="4043" />
If your child does not have an IDP yet, request an IDP assessment.</p>
<p data-end="4151" data-start="4114">You can request a review at any time.</p>
<hr data-end="4156" data-start="4153" />
<h2 data-end="4204" data-start="4158">‚≠ê Step 3 ‚Äî Ask for a multi-agency meeting</h2>
<p data-end="4225" data-start="4205">This should include:</p>
<ul data-end="4385" data-start="4227">
<li data-end="4236" data-start="4227">
<p data-end="4236" data-start="4229"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=ALNCo+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">ALNCo</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="4255" data-start="4237">
<p data-end="4255" data-start="4239"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=LA+ALN+officer+Wales+role&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">LA ALN officer</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="4294" data-start="4256">
<p data-end="4294" data-start="4258">school nurse if health is relevant</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4333" data-start="4295">
<p data-end="4333" data-start="4297">any professionals already involved</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4341" data-start="4334">
<p data-end="4341" data-start="4336">you</p>
</li>
<li data-end="4385" data-start="4342">
<p data-end="4385" data-start="4344">your child if they would like to attend</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="4473" data-start="4387">This is where you explain calmly what has been tried and why it is no longer workable.</p>
<hr data-end="4478" data-start="4475" />
<h2 data-end="4538" data-start="4480">‚≠ê Step 4 ‚Äî Explain clearly why school is not suitable</h2>
<p data-end="4581" data-start="4539">Use simple, factual language, for example:</p>
<blockquote data-end="4675" data-start="4583">
<p data-end="4675" data-start="4585">‚ÄúMy child is unable to access suitable education in a school environment due to [reason].‚Äù</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-end="4744" data-start="4677">Avoid blaming the school or staff.<br data-end="4714" data-start="4711" />
Focus on <strong data-end="4732" data-start="4723">needs</strong>, not fault.</p>
<hr data-end="4749" data-start="4746" />
<h2 data-end="4814" data-start="4751">‚≠ê Step 5 ‚Äî Ask for EOTAS to be considered under Section 19</h2>
<p data-end="4893" data-start="4815">This shows you understand the law and expect a lawful decision-making process.<br /><br /></p><p data-end="450" data-start="39">Summary of relevant Government Documents on the topic <br /><br />In Wales, EOTAS (education otherwise than at school) falls under the <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Education+Act+1996+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">Education Act 1996</a> (as amended for Wales), not the <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=ALN+Act+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">ALN Act</a> or Code.  In particular, <strong data-end="207" data-start="192"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=Section+19A+Education+Act+1996+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">Section 19A</a></strong> of the Education Act 1996 (inserted by the Curriculum and Assessment (Wales) Act 2021) explicitly covers this.  For example, Section&nbsp;19A(1) (titled <em data-end="438" data-start="356">‚ÄúExceptional provision of education in pupil referral units or elsewhere: Wales‚Äù</em>) provides:</p><ul data-end="791" data-start="452">
<li data-end="791" data-start="452">
<p data-end="791" data-start="454"><strong data-end="487" data-start="454">Education Act 1996, s.19A(1):</strong> <em data-end="754" data-start="488">‚ÄúEach local authority in Wales must make arrangements for the provision of suitable education at school or <strong data-end="624" data-start="596">otherwise than at school</strong> for children within the authority‚Äôs area who‚ÄØ‚Ä¶‚ÄØmay not receive suitable education‚ÄØ‚Ä¶‚ÄØunless such arrangements are made for them.‚Äù</em><span data-state="closed"><span class="ms-1 inline-flex max-w-full items-center relative top-[-0.094rem] animate-[show_150ms_ease-in]" data-testid="webpage-citation-pill"><a alt="https://business.senedd.wales/documents/s113694/Curriculum%20and%20Assessment%20Wales%20Bill%20as%20passed.pdf#:~:text=,for%20a%20period%20unless%20such" class="flex h-4.5 overflow-hidden rounded-xl px-2 text-[9px] font-medium transition-colors duration-150 ease-in-out text-token-text-secondary! bg-[#F4F4F4]! dark:bg-[#303030]!" href="https://business.senedd.wales/documents/s113694/Curriculum%20and%20Assessment%20Wales%20Bill%20as%20passed.pdf#:~:text=,for%20a%20period%20unless%20such" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span class="relative start-0 bottom-0 flex h-full w-full items-center">business.senedd.wales</span></a></span></span></p>
</li>
</ul><p data-end="889" data-start="793">Section&nbsp;19A(6) then clarifies that any child in EOTAS is to be treated as a pupil under the Act:</p><ul data-end="1138" data-start="891">
<li data-end="1138" data-start="891">
<p data-end="1138" data-start="893"><strong data-end="926" data-start="893">Education Act 1996, s.19A(6):</strong> <em data-end="1101" data-start="927">‚ÄúThe following persons are to be treated as pupils for the purposes of this Act&nbsp;‚Äì (a) any child for whom education is provided otherwise than at school under this section.‚Äù</em><span data-state="closed"><span class="ms-1 inline-flex max-w-full items-center relative top-[-0.094rem] animate-[show_150ms_ease-in]" data-testid="webpage-citation-pill"><a alt="https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1996/56/section/19A/data.html#:~:text=The%20following%20persons%20are%20to,the%20purposes%20of%20this%20Act%E2%80%94" class="flex h-4.5 overflow-hidden rounded-xl px-2 text-[9px] font-medium transition-colors duration-150 ease-in-out text-token-text-secondary! bg-[#F4F4F4]! dark:bg-[#303030]!" href="https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1996/56/section/19A/data.html#:~:text=The%20following%20persons%20are%20to,the%20purposes%20of%20this%20Act%E2%80%94" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span class="relative start-0 bottom-0 flex h-full w-full items-center">legislation.gov.uk</span></a></span></span></p>
</li>
</ul><p data-end="1448" data-start="1140">These provisions are from the <strong data-end="1192" data-start="1170">Education Act 1996</strong> (as amended in Wales) and form the statutory basis for EOTAS.  The <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=ALN+Code+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">ALN Code</a> for Wales simply notes the duty under Section&nbsp;19 of the Education Act<span data-state="closed"><span class="ms-1 inline-flex max-w-full items-center relative top-[-0.094rem] animate-[show_150ms_ease-in]" data-testid="webpage-citation-pill"><a alt="https://www.gov.wales/sites/default/files/publications/2025-01/250124-the-additional-learning-needs-code-for-wales-2021.pdf#:~:text=Children%20and%20young%20people%20receiving,ill%20or%20injured%20or%20have" class="flex h-4.5 overflow-hidden rounded-xl px-2 text-[9px] font-medium transition-colors duration-150 ease-in-out text-token-text-secondary! bg-[#F4F4F4]! dark:bg-[#303030]!" href="https://www.gov.wales/sites/default/files/publications/2025-01/250124-the-additional-learning-needs-code-for-wales-2021.pdf#:~:text=Children%20and%20young%20people%20receiving,ill%20or%20injured%20or%20have" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span class="relative start-0 bottom-0 flex h-full w-full items-center">gov.wales</span></a></span></span>, but the legal source is Section&nbsp;19A of the Education Act 1996 (Wales).</p><p data-end="4893" data-start="4815">




</p><p data-end="1738" data-is-last-node="" data-is-only-node="" data-start="1450"><strong data-end="1462" data-start="1450">Sources:</strong> Education Act 1996 (c.56), s.19A(1)‚Äì(6)<span data-state="closed"><span class="ms-1 inline-flex max-w-full items-center relative top-[-0.094rem] animate-[show_150ms_ease-in]" data-testid="webpage-citation-pill"><a alt="https://business.senedd.wales/documents/s113694/Curriculum%20and%20Assessment%20Wales%20Bill%20as%20passed.pdf#:~:text=,for%20a%20period%20unless%20such" class="flex h-4.5 overflow-hidden rounded-xl px-2 text-[9px] font-medium transition-colors duration-150 ease-in-out text-token-text-secondary! bg-[#F4F4F4]! dark:bg-[#303030]!" href="https://business.senedd.wales/documents/s113694/Curriculum%20and%20Assessment%20Wales%20Bill%20as%20passed.pdf#:~:text=,for%20a%20period%20unless%20such" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span class="relative start-0 bottom-0 flex h-full w-full items-center">business.senedd.wales</span></a></span></span><span data-state="closed"><span class="ms-1 inline-flex max-w-full items-center relative top-[-0.094rem] animate-[show_150ms_ease-in]" data-testid="webpage-citation-pill"><a alt="https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1996/56/section/19A/data.html#:~:text=The%20following%20persons%20are%20to,the%20purposes%20of%20this%20Act%E2%80%94" class="flex h-4.5 overflow-hidden rounded-xl px-2 text-[9px] font-medium transition-colors duration-150 ease-in-out text-token-text-secondary! bg-[#F4F4F4]! dark:bg-[#303030]!" href="https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1996/56/section/19A/data.html#:~:text=The%20following%20persons%20are%20to,the%20purposes%20of%20this%20Act%E2%80%94" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span class="relative start-0 bottom-0 flex h-full w-full items-center">legislation.gov.uk</span></a></span></span>. <hr data-end="4898" data-start="4895" />
<h1 data-end="4955" data-start="4900">‚≠ê The Most Common Mistake That Leads to EOTAS Refusal</h1>
<p data-end="5128" data-start="4957">Many parents ask for EOTAS too early, before school has attempted the adjustments expected under the ALN Code.<br data-end="5070" data-start="5067" />
This almost always results in refusal and months of delay.</p>
<p data-end="5166" data-start="5130">EOTAS is usually approved <strong data-end="5165" data-start="5156">after</strong>:</p>
<ul data-end="5309" data-start="5168">
<li data-end="5195" data-start="5168">
<p data-end="5195" data-start="5170">adjustments have failed</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5234" data-start="5196">
<p data-end="5234" data-start="5198">reduced timetables have not worked</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5276" data-start="5235">
<p data-end="5276" data-start="5237">reintegration attempts have collapsed</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5309" data-start="5277">
<p data-end="5309" data-start="5279">medical needs are documented</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="5420" data-start="5311">Not because the child is undeserving ‚Äî but because the LA must show they followed the correct legal sequence.</p>
<hr data-end="5425" data-start="5422" />
<h1 data-end="5470" data-start="5427">‚≠ê What Happens After You Make the Request</h1>
<p data-end="5505" data-start="5472">The Local Authority will usually:</p>
<ol data-end="5785" data-start="5507">
<li data-end="5536" data-start="5507">
<p data-end="5536" data-start="5510">acknowledge your request</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5563" data-start="5537">
<p data-end="5563" data-start="5540">arrange an IDP Review</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5589" data-start="5564">
<p data-end="5589" data-start="5567">look at the evidence</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5636" data-start="5590">
<p data-end="5636" data-start="5593">ask the school for additional information</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5670" data-start="5637">
<p data-end="5670" data-start="5640">consider further adjustments</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5717" data-start="5671">
<p data-end="5717" data-start="5674">arrange short term tuition if appropriate</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5755" data-start="5718">
<p data-end="5755" data-start="5721">send the case to the EOTAS Panel</p>
</li>
<li data-end="5785" data-start="5756">
<p data-end="5785" data-start="5759">issue a written decision</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p data-end="5879" data-start="5787">If EOTAS is approved, the LA must arrange suitable provision as soon as reasonably possible.</p>
<p data-end="5908" data-start="5881">If refused, continue below.</p>
<hr data-end="5913" data-start="5910" />
<h1 data-end="5957" data-start="5915">‚≠ê If the Local Authority Refuses EOTAS</h1>
<p data-end="5998" data-start="5958">A refusal is not the end of the process.</p>
<p data-end="6024" data-start="6000">Here is what to do next:</p>
<h2 data-end="6066" data-start="6026">‚≠ê 1. Ask for the refusal in writing</h2>
<p data-end="6120" data-start="6067">This prevents confusion about what was actually said.</p>
<hr data-end="6125" data-start="6122" />
<h2 data-end="6169" data-start="6127">‚≠ê 2. Ask what evidence they relied on</h2>
<p data-end="6213" data-start="6170">This identifies gaps and misunderstandings.</p>
<hr data-end="6218" data-start="6215" />
<h2 data-end="6256" data-start="6220">‚≠ê 3. Request another IDP Review</h2>
<p data-end="6303" data-start="6257">There is no limit on how many you can request.</p>
<hr data-end="6308" data-start="6305" />
<h2 data-end="6346" data-start="6310">‚≠ê 4. Gather additional evidence</h2>
<p data-end="6415" data-start="6347">A short update from the GP or school nurse can shift an entire case.</p>
<hr data-end="6420" data-start="6417" />
<h2 data-end="6459" data-start="6422">‚≠ê 5. Keep a log of deterioration</h2>
<p data-end="6482" data-start="6460">Patterns are powerful:</p>
<ul data-end="6609" data-start="6484">
<li data-end="6497" data-start="6484">
<p data-end="6497" data-start="6486">shutdowns</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6511" data-start="6498">
<p data-end="6511" data-start="6500"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=autistic+meltdowns&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">meltdowns</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="6521" data-start="6512">
<p data-end="6521" data-start="6514"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=panic+attack+management+strategies&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">panic</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="6533" data-start="6522">
<p data-end="6533" data-start="6524">fatigue</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6554" data-start="6534">
<p data-end="6554" data-start="6536">sensory overload</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6575" data-start="6555">
<p data-end="6575" data-start="6557"><a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=child+sleep+disruption+causes&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">sleep disruption</a></p>
</li>
<li data-end="6609" data-start="6576">
<p data-end="6609" data-start="6578">failed reintegration attempts</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="6660" data-start="6611">Logs are often more persuasive than long reports.</p>
<hr data-end="6665" data-start="6662" />
<h2 data-end="6714" data-start="6667">‚≠ê 6. Request interim medical needs tuition</h2>
<p data-end="6787" data-start="6715">Your child cannot be left without education while decisions are pending.</p>
<hr data-end="6792" data-start="6789" />
<h2 data-end="6826" data-start="6794">‚≠ê 7. Challenge the decision</h2>
<p data-end="6835" data-start="6827">You can:</p>
<ul data-end="6964" data-start="6837">
<li data-end="6864" data-start="6837">
<p data-end="6864" data-start="6839">ask for reconsideration</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6887" data-start="6865">
<p data-end="6887" data-start="6867">submit a complaint</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6914" data-start="6888">
<p data-end="6914" data-start="6890">escalate within the LA</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6941" data-start="6915">
<p data-end="6941" data-start="6917">use dispute resolution</p>
</li>
<li data-end="6964" data-start="6942">
<p data-end="6964" data-start="6944">appeal to <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=education+tribunal+Wales&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">Tribunal</a></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="7164" data-start="6966">Most specialist interventions (such as Intensive Interaction, full online packages or <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=define+ABA+therapy&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=969600017788725838" target="_blank">ABA</a>) only become available when a Tribunal orders them.<br data-end="7110" data-start="7107" />
These are rare and usually require extensive evidence.</p>
<hr data-end="7169" data-start="7166" />
<h1 data-end="7205" data-start="7171">‚≠ê When Tribunal May Be Necessary</h1>
<p data-end="7236" data-start="7207">Tribunal is appropriate when:</p>
<ul data-end="7524" data-start="7238">
<li data-end="7280" data-start="7238">
<p data-end="7280" data-start="7240">your child cannot attend school safely</p>
</li>
<li data-end="7338" data-start="7281">
<p data-end="7338" data-start="7283">the LA insists on reintegration with no evidence base</p>
</li>
<li data-end="7374" data-start="7339">
<p data-end="7374" data-start="7341">medical needs are being ignored</p>
</li>
<li data-end="7410" data-start="7375">
<p data-end="7410" data-start="7377">reduced timetables are drifting</p>
</li>
<li data-end="7444" data-start="7411">
<p data-end="7444" data-start="7413">interim tuition is inadequate</p>
</li>
<li data-end="7480" data-start="7445">
<p data-end="7480" data-start="7447">no school placement is suitable</p>
</li>
<li data-end="7524" data-start="7481">
<p data-end="7524" data-start="7483">EOTAS is clearly the only lawful option</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="7594" data-start="7526">Tribunals focus on evidence.<br data-end="7557" data-start="7554" />
Build your case carefully and calmly.</p>
