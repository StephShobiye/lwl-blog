<h2 data-end="3643" data-start="3601">Support from Learn Without Limits CIC</h2>
<p data-end="3860" data-start="3644">If you are affected by flooding, please <strong data-end="3695" data-start="3684">tell us</strong> in the Facebook group.<br />https://www.facebook.com/groups/learnwithoutlimitscic<br /><br data-end="3721" data-start="3718" />
We cannot promise the world, and we cannot give what we do not have, but we <em data-end="3802" data-start="3797">can</em> try to support our fellow ALN families in any way we can.</p>
<p data-end="4159" data-start="3862">Sometimes another parent can lend or donate a temporary item.<br data-end="3926" data-start="3923" />
Sometimes someone nearby has spare incontinence supplies.<br data-end="3986" data-start="3983" />
Sometimes a member has an old wheelchair or stroller they no longer need.<br data-end="4062" data-start="4059" />
Sometimes you may need help writing a <a data-preview="" href="https://www.google.com/search?ved=1t:260882&amp;q=define+social+story&amp;bbid=6470633950786405900&amp;bpid=8182776451391397419" target="_blank">social story</a>, completing a form or finding local support.</p>
<p data-end="4230" data-start="4161">And sometimes all you need is a safe space to vent without judgment.</p>
<p data-end="4343" data-start="4232">You do not have to face this alone.<br data-end="4270" data-start="4267" />
This community was built to support one another in exactly these moments.</p>
