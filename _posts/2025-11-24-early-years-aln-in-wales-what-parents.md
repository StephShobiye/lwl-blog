<p data-end="9889" data-start="9833">These simple steps create safety, stability, and insight.</p>

<h1 data-end="9916" data-start="9896"><strong data-end="9916" data-start="9898">Final Thoughts</strong></h1>
<p data-end="10019" data-start="9918">Early years support in Wales is not about labels ‚Äî it‚Äôs about understanding the child in front of us.</p>
<p data-end="10216" data-start="10021">Whether a child has a diagnosed disability, an emerging need, a chronic health condition, sensory differences, or unexplained distress, they deserve compassion, safety, and access to education now.</p>
