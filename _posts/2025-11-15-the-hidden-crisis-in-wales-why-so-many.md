</li>
<li data-end="2489" data-start="2440">

<h2 data-end="7018" data-start="6964">9. Why this matters for Welsh policy and practice</h2>
<p data-end="7372" data-start="7084">Wales cannot close the attainment gap, improve attendance, or deliver genuine equity without addressing the emotional well-being crisis.<br data-end="7221" data-start="7218" />
The evidence is clear: when children feel safe, supported and understood, everything improves — learning, attendance, behaviour and long-term outcomes.</p>
<p data-end="7501" data-start="7374">Investing in ALN recognition, trauma-informed practice, and early mental health intervention is not optional.<br data-end="7485" data-start="7482" />
It is essential.</p>
